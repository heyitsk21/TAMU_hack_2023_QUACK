Technology for Kids - Robotics

Welcome to the Technology Corner for Kids!

What's a robot's favorite food? ... Microchips!

Robotics is a fascinating field that involves engineering and computer science to design, build, and operate robots, and is just one branch of technology.

Robotics is used in many different fields, like manufacturing, healthcare, agriculture, construction, security, etc.

Additionally, there are parts of technology called software and hardware! Software is the set of instructions which enable the hardware to perform a specific set of tasks. Software was used to create this website! Hardware is the physical and visible components of the system.

Technology has become a vital part of our lives, and allows us to be connected throughout the world. Usually, you don't realize how much technology you use in your daily life, but technology is the reason you can access this website. While there is still plenty of room for improvement, technology has advanced a lot in the past few decades and is irreplaceable in society.
